Meri Jaan,

Agar tum yeh padh rahi ho, iska matlab tumne mujh par bharosa kiya.

Listen to me carefully. I know you are scared. I know raaton ko rona aata hai. I know tumhe lagta hai Mummy humein alag kar dengi.

But trust me on this: Main sambhaal loonga.

Yeh situation mushkil hai, par namumkin nahi. Main tumhe khone nahi dunga. Chahe kuch bhi ho jaye, main hamesha tumhare saath khada hoon. Just hold my hand tight.

Log bolenge, family bolegi, duniya bolegi. Par unki awaaz humare pyaar se badi nahi hai.

Rona band karo. Smile karo. Kyunki tumhari smile meri taakat hai.

I love you. Infinite times.


- Tera Bhondhu
